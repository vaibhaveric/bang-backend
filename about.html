Our story · since 1987

Three generations.
One recipe book.

From a single wood-fired tava behind Mahveer Ganj market in 1987, to a heritage kitchen serving 410+ pin codes across India. The recipes, the people, and the smell of fresh khoya have stayed exactly the same.

Our kitchen

Built by hand.
Cut by hand.

Every kaju katli is still rolled out by Bhaiya Ram and his team — the same family that started Bangali Sweets in 1987. We don't use machines for shaping. We don't use vanaspati. And we don't use yesterday's chenna.

What we do use: pure cow ghee from local Bhind dairies, hand-ground cashews, Kashmiri kesar, fresh khoya delivered every morning, and three generations of muscle memory.

It takes longer. It costs more. It tastes the way it should.

Our journey

Forty years in the making

1987

A counter behind Mahveer Ganj

Bhaiya Ram opens a 4×6 ft mithai counter in Pustak Bazar. Six recipes. One tava. Customers walk in for kaju katli on Diwali — by 1990, queues stretch into the lane.

2004

The next generation joins

Ramesh ji takes over day-to-day. We add namkeen and dairy. Open a proper kitchen two doors down. FSSAI registration in 2008.

2019

Pan-India shipping

Sealed, vacuum-packed mithai begins shipping to Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore. Bhind diaspora gets the taste of home, anywhere.

2026

Online, properly.

Bangalisweets.in launches with same-day Bhind delivery, pan-India shipping, and a corporate gifting programme. The recipes haven't changed. The reach has.

Shipping & delivery

Order online, pick up from our Pustak Bazar outlet. Always FREE. We'll WhatsApp you when ready — usually within 30 minutes.

Pin code not deliverable?

We deliver to 410+ pin codes. If yours isn't covered, WhatsApp us — for premium orders we arrange a direct courier.

Returns & freshness

What if my mithai isn't perfect?

WhatsApp us a photo within 24 hours of delivery. We re-ship a fresh batch the same day at our cost. No paperwork. No questions.

Cancellation

Cancel free of charge up to 1 hour before dispatch. After dispatch, no cancellations on perishable items.

Storage instructions

Most mithai: 5–10 days at room temperature in a sealed container. Khoya-based items (kalakand, rasgulla): refrigerate within 2 days. Each pack carries item-specific instructions.

FAQs

Is everything pure vegetarian?

Yes. We have been a 100% vegetarian kitchen since day one. No eggs in mithai. Our bakery has eggless options clearly marked.

Our Anjeer Roll is naturally sweetened with figs and dates — no added sugar. On request we can prepare sugar-free versions of select mithai for diabetic customers (5-day lead time).

Can I customise a hamper?

Yes. WhatsApp us your selection and we'll build a custom box. Minimum order ₹999. Add notes, ribbons, custom cards.

Do you cater weddings?

Yes — full mithai & namkeen catering for weddings in Bhind / Gwalior region. WhatsApp 0797 4096 667 for our wedding rate card.
